Building in public works, but the personal brand grind is the part most developers hate. Bip keeps a log of your real dev work while you code, then turns it into honest build-in-public posts in your own words. No face. No pitch. No marketing voice. Just the work, written down.

It is not ready yet. I am building Bip in the open right now. It is not something you can download today. Leave your email and I will send you one message, the day you can actually try it. That is the whole deal. No newsletter, no drip sequence, no selling.

Made for developers who build on the side and would rather not sell themselves online. One email, only when it is real.
